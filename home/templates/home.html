Overview

The Centre for Innovation Incubation and Entrepreneurship (CIIE)
was started to encourage participation in the field of
entrepreneurship and innovation. It works to promote the
sharing of new ideas between ingenious minds and gives them
a platform to collaborate.

Vision

CIIE views to build an Entrepreneurial Ecosystem to
provide a platform for students with creative minds and
ideas and the potential to create business
opportunities.

Mission

CIIE aims to create awareness about Entrepreneurship
and develop such skills among the students.
It intends to nurture the budding entrepreneurs
within NU and provide them with equalopportunities.
